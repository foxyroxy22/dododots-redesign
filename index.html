  • How long does it take to see results
    when using Dododots?

    Yes, our patches are suitable for all skin types, whether acne-prone, sensitive, oily, dry, combination or normal.

  • Can it work on all types of acne?

    As with all skincare, our pimple patches will work differently on different types of skin and acne.
    Our patches work best on pus-filled acne such as papules and pustules. Customers have also reported that our patches flattened their newly-formed acne after ONE day of usage.
    This does not apply to closed comedones, milia and cystic acne. For these types of acne, our patches can help to cover and protect the skin from environmental triggers thus prevent further inflammation.

  • Is it suitable for sensitive skin?

    Some customers see results in 1-2 days, while others see results after 1 week of using Dododots. As with most skincare products, results differ with different individuals with different skin types.
